Project 03 / Competition robotics

VEX Over Under

A competition robot and team program that converted mechanical versatility, autonomous control and disciplined preparation into back-to-back Australian titles.

RoleCaptain + lead programmer
Team7 people / 41103A
Timeline2018 - 2024
Result2x national champion
The challenge

Build a robot that could score, traverse and climb - reliably.

The Over Under game demanded triball handling, movement across a central barrier and an end-game elevation climb. The robot needed mechanical flexibility and predictable autonomous positioning under competition conditions.

Control stack

Fuse multiple sensors into repeatable field positioning.

IMUHeading feedback for turns and orientation.
EncodersMotor and drivetrain movement feedback.
Colour sensorAdditional field-state input.
C++ controlAutonomous and driver-control behaviour.
My contribution

Own the code, shape the mechanism and lead the team.

  • Program the full control stack. Wrote autonomous and driver-control code in C++.
  • Design pneumatic mechanisms. Developed intake, scoring and elevation-climb systems.
  • Lead competition execution. Managed strategy, driver training and preparation for a seven-person team.
  • Build capability around the team. Mentored junior teams and helped organise the 2022 Australian National VEX Championship.
