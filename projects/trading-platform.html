Project 06 / Financial software

Trading Platform

A modular Python system for market data, order execution and portfolio management with risk controls and historical validation built into the architecture.

LanguagePython
BrokerInteractive Brokers
Timeline2025 - present
StatusBacktesting
The brief

Make strategy development prove itself before it can touch capital.

The platform is designed around separation of concerns: market data, strategy logic, risk management, execution and portfolio state. Strategies must pass historical testing before live deployment is considered.

Architecture

Risk is a system component, not an afterthought.

Market dataReal-time and historical feeds through the IBKR API.
StrategyModular trading logic evaluated against incoming data.
Risk controlsPosition sizing, stop-loss logic and drawdown caps.
ExecutionOrder management and portfolio updates.
Engineering decisions

Design for validation and containment.

  • Keep the system modular. Data, strategies, risk and execution can evolve independently.
  • Make risk explicit. Position sizing, stops and drawdown limits are part of the core workflow.
  • Backtest first. Historical validation gates any move toward live capital.
  • Use a production broker interface. IBKR provides real market data and execution APIs.
Current status

The platform is in backtesting and validation. No live-performance claim is made; this page will add test methodology and results once they are robust enough to publish.
